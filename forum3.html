Anybody shed any light on whether the 16-85 was ever offered as a kit lens, and if so what with?

I think you need to think very carefully about how important that extra 2mm is at the wide end - it doesn't sound much, but it really is a significant difference. If you want that extra width, I would suggest you think about body only and get the 16-85 separately. I don't think it's likely to be cost effective to get a D7000+18-105 bundle, and then add a 16-85 as well.

And if anybody wonders how I come to have both the 16-85 and 18-140 in my gear, a) it's a long story and b) neither were bought as part of a kit!
